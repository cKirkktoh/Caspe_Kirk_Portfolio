About Me

I am a Filipino born in Saudi Arabia and have a strong interest in photo and video editing. I graduated from high school and attended Fanshawe College's Interactive Media Design Program. Initially clueless, my skills in each category improved over time, and now I specialize in logo designing. Currently, I am a college student specializing in Interactive Media Design at Fanshawe's London Downtown Campus. Armed with skills in web design, programming, and 3-D animation, I look forward to helping many clients in the future.
