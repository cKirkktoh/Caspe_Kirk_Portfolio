Mini Advertisement Campaign

Overview

The Mini Advertising campaign video that me and my groupmates created aims to address a pressing public health issue: informing teenagers about the potential risks and effects of e-cigarettes (vaping) or marijuana. Grounded in Health Canada's mandate to promote health and safety, the campaign video utilizes powerful visuals, compelling storytelling, and factual information to raise awareness among its target audience. By highlighting the potential health consequences associated with vaping or marijuana use, the video seeks to empower teenagers to make informed decisions about their well-being. Through a blend of creativity and advocacy, this campaign video serves as a crucial tool in educating and protecting young individuals from the harms of substance abuse.

Goals

  • Raise Awareness: The primary goal of the mini advertising campaign video is to raise awareness among teenagers about the potential health risks and consequences associated with the use of e-cigarettes (vaping) or marijuana.
  • Educate: The video aims to educate teenagers about the specific health effects and dangers of vaping or marijuana use, providing factual information backed by scientific research to empower them to make informed decisions about their health and well-being.
  • Promote Prevention: Another goal of the campaign video is to encourage teenagers to avoid or refrain from using e-cigarettes or marijuana altogether, emphasizing the importance of prioritizing their health and making positive choices for their future.
