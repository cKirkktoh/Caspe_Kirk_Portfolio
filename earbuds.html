Technical Specifications

Driver Type
Dynamic driver
The driver type determines the sound quality and characteristics of the earbuds. Dynamic drivers are known for their deep bass and clear audio performance.
Frequency Response
20Hz - 20kHz
The frequency response range indicates the audio frequencies the earbuds can reproduce. 20Hz - 20kHz covers a wide range of sounds from deep bass to crisp highs.
Sensitivity
95dB
Sensitivity measures how efficiently the earbuds convert electrical signals into sound. A sensitivity of 95dB ensures loud and clear audio output.
Impedance
32 ohms
Impedance indicates the opposition to the flow of electrical current. A lower impedance, such as 32 ohms, allows the earbuds to work well with a wide range of devices.
