Print AD Campaign

Overview

Through creative headline and tagline development, as well as compelling visual elements, groups will craft three distinct advertisements to be pitched in class. The objective is to generate awareness of the discount offer while effectively challenging societal norms and perceptions, ultimately positioning EasyTone as a desirable and inclusive option for men seeking comfort and style in their footwear choices. Through innovative messaging and visually impactful designs, each group will strive to capture the attention of their audience and convey the brand's commitment to both quality and inclusivity.

We initially spent a lot of time doing v arious advertising research, referring to advertisements of different sports brands and their design concepts. This project requires men in the age gr oup of 30 to 50. The fir st thing we thought of was the role of a father. Most people in this age gr oup are married and have children; the second character is an office worker who is obese due to busy work and no time to exercise; the third character is a man who is fat and has no time t o workout to do yoga, but then realizes that working out would change his life. Generally speaking, we want to create a sense of contrast, let a character have different physical states, and also want to promote product sales in this w ay.

Goals

  • Create awareness of 25% off discount while removing stigma of men wearing EasyTone.
  • Challenge Stigma: Another goal is to challenge and dismantle any existing stigma surrounding men wearing EasyTone footwear. The campaign aims to convey a message of inclusivity and acceptance, encouraging men to embrace the product without fear of judgment or societal norms.
